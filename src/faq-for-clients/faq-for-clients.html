What is Venue Genie?

Booking events can be both stressful and tedious. You don't know which venues offer event space and you don’t want to research, call and email 100 venues for more information. Venue Genie quickly connects you toour network of venues. Save time and focus on what really matters -- throwing a successful event.

This is why we’ve created an online marketplace that helps users find the right place to celebrate their event - based on their preferences - in a clever way, using the genie’s magic.
